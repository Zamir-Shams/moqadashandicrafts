About Us

Our Mission

Our mission is to promote cultural exchange and understanding between the East and West by combining their unique art forms through the creation and sale of handicrafts. We believe that by fostering collaboration and mutual respect between these two distinct cultures, we can create a better future for all. We are committed to empowering Afghan women by providing them with the resources and training necessary to achieve self-sufficiency. Additionally, we recognize the value of art in promoting cultural expression and pride, and aim to support Afghan women in this field as well.

Our Vision

We, MOQADAS Handicraft Center at Roma, Italy are a popular firm that ideates, creates and sells a wide range of handicraft items. The handicraft culture never goes out of style and we make sure to stay up-to-date with designs and products coming in the market. We aim to stay on top of the market and be the best amongst our competitors. We sell products that are high on satisfaction, durability and style, all at once. Contact us, we'll show you our work and esteemed clientele.
